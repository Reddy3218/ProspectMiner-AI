# AI Lead Scoring Strategy

ProspectMiner AI evaluates leads based on multiple signals.

## Scoring Factors

- Website presence
- Google rating
- Number of reviews
- Keyword relevance
- Business category

## Score Levels

| Score | Level |
|------|------|
| 70+ | High |
| 40–69 | Medium |
| <40 | Low |

This scoring system helps prioritize outreach targets.
